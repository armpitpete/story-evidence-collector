# Power Profile notes

## Subject

UK political leadership candidate or MP

## Research question

What does this political figure say they stand for, and what interests sit close to their power?

## Known sources from intake

- UK Parliament profile,Register of Members' Financial Interests,voting record,Electoral Commission donations,Companies House,official website,public appointment records,reputable reporting

## Wanted output

- article,evidence table,source links,connection chart,what is proven,what is not proven,TWIS judgement

## Sensitivity

sensitive

## Safety rule

Simple at the front.

Structured underneath.

Human-checkable proof trail throughout.

## Do not include

- private family material
- gossip
- rumours
- unevidenced friendships
- psychological claims
- private relationships
- guilt-by-association claims

Every public chart edge must have a source link.
